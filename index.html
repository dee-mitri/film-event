Our Flatiron Studio

Located in the creative heart of New York's Flatiron District, our studio serves as a sanctuary for visual storytellers. We created this space to tear down the barriers between emerging talent and the resources they need.

Tonight, our professional soundstages are transformed into an intimate cinema, providing a platform for filmmakers to screen their unique, unfiltered visions to an audience of industry peers and cinema lovers.

The Vanguard Award

We believe in actively investing in the next generation of cinema. That is why this isn't just a screening—it's a launchpad.

One standout filmmaker will be honored with the Nocturnal Vanguard Award.

The winner will be granted free, exclusive use of our Flatiron studio location for any future shooting project, providing the high-end production value your next vision deserves.

Call For Entries

Requirements

We are looking for narrative-driven works that push technical and creative boundaries.

  • Duration: Minimum 15 Minutes.
  • Resolution: 1920x1080 or 4K
  • Format: High-res .MP4 or .MOV.
  • Exclusivity: Finalists must be present in NYC.
  • Broadcast: Must grant streaming rights.

Footage must be submitted via our official portal.
